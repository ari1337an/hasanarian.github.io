Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, medical imaging, and healthcare. I am exploring research questions such as: "Can we improve deep neural networks by designing their architectures inspired by the human brain?" and "To what extent can we apply AI-driven automation in healthcare?"
